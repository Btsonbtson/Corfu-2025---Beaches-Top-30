Corfu Travel Guide 2025

Welcome to Corfu Town

Corfu Town combines historical depth with cosmopolitan charm. Discover Venetian architecture, walk around the Liston promenade, and explore the majestic Old Fortress.

  • Landmarks: Saint Spyridon Church, Palace of St. Michael and St. George, Old and New Fortresses
  • Museums: Archaeological Museum, Museum of Asian Art, Byzantine Museum
  • Hidden Corners: Campiello alleys, Boschetto Garden, Old Jewish Quarter

Enjoy local cuisine in traditional taverns, sip a drink at rooftop bars, and experience the nightlife under the Corfiot sky.

Benitses

A charming seaside village combining lush mountain scenery with authentic Corfiot culture. Ideal for relaxation and exploration alike.
