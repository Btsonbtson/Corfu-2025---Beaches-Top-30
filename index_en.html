Corfu Town

Corfu Town is one of the most charming cities in Greece. With its blend of Venetian, French and British influences, it enchants visitors with its architecture, narrow alleys, historic squares, and vibrant life. The Old Fortress, Liston, Spianada Square, the Palace of St. Michael and St. George, and the numerous churches form a vivid mosaic of history and culture. Ideal for walking, shopping, and dining with local delicacies.

Benitses

Benitses is a traditional fishing village located a few kilometers south of Corfu Town. It combines natural beauty with tourism development while maintaining its authentic character. You can stroll around the old village with narrow paths, stone houses, and flower-filled courtyards. The sea is calm and ideal for families, while the marina offers options for sailing and excursions.
