Benitses – Corfu 2025

Your guide to a peaceful, authentic village

🍽️ Where to Eat

The village has many restaurants and tavernas serving everything from traditional Greek cuisine to Chinese and Mexican dishes.

  • Pizza Kavouras – Ask for Spyros or Gregory
  • Oraies Benitses – Ask for Dimitris
  • Paxinos – Specializing in Fresh Fish
  • Klimataria – Special Dish: Bianco
  • GyrosLeague – Souvlaki to go

🗺️ What to See

  • Achilleion Palace – About 4km away by car. Go early to avoid tourist crowds.
  • Explore local agencies offering boat and cave excursions.
  • Daily trip to Erikousa island – A paradise if you have enough days.

🎉 Nightlife

In the evening, you can enjoy a meal at one of the many restaurants and then move on to a pub or bar for cocktails or music. Benitses nightlife is not overly intense, but there are some nice places:

  • Ether – Cozy bar
  • Lotza – Popular gathering spot
  • Marina Bar – Chill drinks by the sea
  • Angsana Hotel – For premium cocktails and breathtaking views

💡 Local Tips

  • It’s common for locals to greet you or even ask about Sonia (my mother), Sofia (my sister), or me – don’t be surprised!
  • Park your car at the municipal parking lot 150m uphill near the basketball court (locals call it “Drafo”).
  • That’s also where trash is collected daily – keep it clean.
  • Leave the ducks alone.
  • From the house, past the court, you can start a 4.5 km hiking loop with amazing views. Do it early in the morning, not at midday!
  • Coffee? Everywhere. And it's good.
