Benitses – Corfu 2025

Your personal guide to a charming fishing village in Corfu

About Benitses

Benitses is a charming fishing village on the east coast of Corfu, known for its authenticity and lively seaside tavernas. It's a blend of local tradition and touristic comfort with a warm and welcoming vibe.

🍽️ Where to Eat

The village offers a wide variety of restaurants and tavernas serving traditional Greek food as well as Chinese and Mexican dishes.

  • Pizza Kavouras – Ask for Spyros or Gregory
  • Oraies Benitses – Ask for Dimitris
  • Paxinos – Specializing in fresh fish
  • Klimataria – Signature dish: Bianco
  • GyrosLeague – Great souvlaki to go

🏛️ Sightseeing

  • Achilleion Palace: Only 4 km away by car. Go early to avoid crowds.
  • Local tour offices: Organize boat trips and tours with highlights like sea caves.
  • Recommended: Day cruise to Erikousa Island – A hidden paradise! 🌴

🎉 Entertainment & Nightlife

In the evening, you can choose from various tavernas and enjoy a cocktail at a seaside bar. While Benitses doesn't have intense nightlife, it offers some stylish bars:

  • Ether, Loza, and the bar at the Marina
  • Agsana Hotel: For upscale drinks and breathtaking views

💡 Local Tips

  • Locals might greet you or even walk into the yard to say "kalimera" – it's normal! 😊
  • You can park at the free municipal lot ("Drafo") ~150m uphill, next to the basketball court.
  • Garbage collection happens daily near the same parking spot.
  • Don’t disturb the ducks. 🦆
  • Behind the basketball court starts a beautiful 4.5 km hiking trail with panoramic views.
  • Prefer morning walks to avoid midday sun.
  • Excellent coffee is available everywhere!
